Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How long does the coverage process take?

Your press will be published across our media network within 5-7 business days from submission.

Are the placements guaranteed?

Each plan includes a set number of guaranteed features on top-tier outlets. Beyond these, we distribute your press to hundreds more reputable publishers, though their acceptance may vary. You'll always receive a detailed report of all guaranteed placements.

What kind of press can I place?

Company news, product launches, business milestones, appointments, and other newsworthy business announcements can be placed.

Do you help with writing?

While our focus is on securing coverage, we're happy to offer editorial guidance and feedback on your press release. This helps ensure it resonates with journalists and audiences worldwide.

What is ElevateAI technology?

ElevateAI is our proprietary technology that formats and positions your content specifically for integration into AI knowledge systems. When people ask questions through AI chatbots about topics related to your announcement, your content becomes a potential reference source—creating ongoing visibility long after publication.

Which AI platforms will index my content?

With our Elite Global Coverage plan, your content is formatted for major AI platforms that browse the web, including popular AI chatbots and search tools. While we optimize your content for these systems, the specific AI platforms may vary based on their respective data policies.

Is podcast distribution included in all plans?

Podcast distribution is automatically included in our Elite Global Coverage plan. This includes conversion of your press release into audio format and distribution across 8 major podcast platforms including Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Amazon Music, Player.fm, iHeart, Podchaser, Podbean, and Boomplay.
