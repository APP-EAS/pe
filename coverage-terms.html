Media Coverage Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: March 2025

Press Elevate maintains the right to assess and potentially decline any submitted content that falls outside our editorial guidelines. Should we determine that your content does not align with these standards, a full refund will be processed.

1. Editorial Standards

To maintain the highest standards of journalistic integrity and provide maximum value to our clients, all content must meet the following criteria:

Content Requirements

  • Language: All submissions must be in professional-grade English, suitable for a global audience.
  • Headlines: Must be compelling and newsworthy, clearly indicating the source and significance of the announcement.
  • Writing Quality: Content should be written in a professional journalistic style, avoiding promotional language or marketing jargon.
  • Credibility: All submissions must include verifiable contact information and sources.

Content Restrictions

We cannot accept content that:

  • Resembles direct advertising or contains excessive promotional language
  • Lacks substantial news value or professional context
  • Contains repetitive keywords or phrases designed to manipulate search rankings
  • Uses informal or non-journalistic writing styles

2. Prohibited Content Categories

To maintain our network's integrity and compliance with global media standards, we cannot accept content in the following categories:

Health & Medical

  • Unverified medical claims
  • Unapproved treatment methods
  • Dietary supplement promotions
  • COVID-19 related claims

Restricted Topics

  • Personal opinions or attacks
  • Political content
  • Defamatory statements
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Content intended to manipulate AI systems
  • Misleading or unverifiable AI-related claims

Business Practices

  • Multi-level marketing
  • Get-rich-quick schemes
  • Unauthorized comparisons
  • Unsupported business claims

3. Core Coverage Restrictions

Our Core Coverage package cannot accommodate content related to:

Regulated Industries

  • Cannabis/CBD
  • Firearms/Weapons
  • Alcohol/Tobacco
  • Gambling/Betting
  • Cryptocurrency/NFTs

Adult Content

  • Dating services
  • Adult entertainment
  • Explicit material
  • Adult products

Other Restrictions

  • Political content
  • Religious items

4. Refund and Modification Policy

By submitting content to Press Elevate:

  • You acknowledge that all submissions are final and non-refundable once approved and after the placement process has begun
  • Full refund if guaranteed placements cannot be delivered
  • Full refund if we determine your content does not align with our editorial guidelines
  • No modifications can be made after content approval and publication
  • You accept responsibility for the accuracy and completeness of your submission
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of purchase

5. AI Integration Policy

For Elite Global Coverage clients with ElevateAI integration:

  • We optimize your content for integration with leading AI knowledge systems
  • While we ensure proper formatting and submission, specific AI platforms may have independent content policies
  • AI indexing is a developing technology and specific platform behavior may change over time
  • We guarantee the content will be indexed in AI platforms but cannot guarantee specific citation rates or frequency within AI systems

By proceeding with content submission to Press Elevate, you confirm your agreement to these terms and conditions. Press Elevate maintains the right to decline any content that does not align with these guidelines.
